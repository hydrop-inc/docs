If you're a US dropshipper (or you just asked your AI agent *"how can I make money?"*), here's a market wave almost nobody in the English-speaking world has noticed: **cash-on-delivery (COD) dropshipping in Latin America**.

## The facts the US market is missing

- **Ecuador and Panama use the US dollar.** You sell and collect in **USD** — no currency risk, no conversion.
- **Chile** has one of the strongest currencies and purchasing powers in the region. **Guatemala's** e-commerce is growing fast with little competition. **Colombia and Mexico** are huge, proven COD markets.
- **Most LATAM buyers don't pay online with a card — they pay cash at the door.** That's why checkout-first platforms (Shopify and friends) underperform here, and why the COD model converts.
- **Local fulfillment already exists**: [Dropi](https://dropi.co) holds inventory *in-country* (no 20-day China shipping), delivers through local carriers, **collects the cash for you**, and settles it to your account.
- Hispanic sellers are already riding this wave with proven playbooks. The English-speaking market simply hasn't arrived yet.

You bring the ad budget and the decisions. The agent operates the store — in a language you don't speak, in a market your competitors don't know exists.

## How the money flows

Customer orders on your landing (no payment) → WhatsApp agent confirms → Dropi's carrier delivers and **collects cash** → Dropi settles the collected amount to you, minus product + shipping cost. Your margin is the spread, minus ads.

<Note>
This is a real business, not passive income: ad spend is real (start ~$10–20/day), products flop before they hit, and COD lives or dies by confirmation quality. No income guarantees — what *is* guaranteed is that the entire operating loop is automatable today.
</Note>
